Welcome to the World of Orcas

Where are Orcas found and why are they famous?

Orcas, also known as killer whales, are found in oceans around the world from the Arctic and Antarctic regions to tropical seas. They are famous for their striking black-and-white coloration, incredible intelligence, complex social structures, and status as apex predators.
